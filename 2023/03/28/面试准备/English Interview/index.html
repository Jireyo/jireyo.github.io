English Interview

 在面试中，我们很想了解更多关于你的背景、经历、兴趣以及你的工作动力，所以我们建议你重新熟悉一下你的申请和招聘广告。也会有充足的时间让你们提问。

感谢面试机会

Thank you for your participation.

  • It’s my pleasure to attend the interview.
  • Thank you for giving me the opportunity to interview.
  • I am grateful for the opportunity to interview with you.

最近怎么样

How are you doing？

  • I’m doing really well.

Did you do anything interesting over the weekend?

  • I’m studied this weekend. But I got a lot done, so that was good.

自我介绍

校园背景

Tell me sth about you？

  • My name is Jin Renyu, 23 years old, and I come from Hubei province. I’m currently studying for my master’s degree in mechanical engineering at Huazhong University of Science and Technology.I am expected to graduate in June 2024.
  • During my university studies, I discovered my interest in coding. Compared to other courses that required extensive experimental conditions, coding allowed me to easily and quickly bring my ideas to life, which is why I started dedicating time to learning programming.
  • In grad school, I worked with my fellow students in the laboratory to complete several Internet of Things projects, which benefit me a lot.

Why didn’t you get a master’s in computer science?

  • Because I had already got the qualification to be recommended to my graduate school, pursuing a computer science postgraduate program was not a cost-effective option.

Which course did you find it difficult to obtain experimental conditions？

  • There was a course called “Digital Design of Electromechanical Products” in which my team and I designed an electric wheelchair. We designed the structure and did some calculations to make it stable in multiple gears. Unfortunately, it was only a design and we didn’t have the means to manufacture or test it.

How did you get interested in programming?

  • One day, I found out that one of my classmates had created his own learning blog. I was inspired and wanted to have a personal webpage that I could freely customize, so I started learning programming.

Can I have a look at you blog?

  • Sorry, I haven’t maintained it for a long time and I’m not sure if it’s still working properly.

个人优势

What is your greatest strength？

  • I would like to say that my biggest strength is my strong ability to self-study and adapt. When I enter a new environment, like when I first joined the lab at graduate school , I often feel uncomfortable because I worry about not being able to keep up with others’ progress or being accepted by them. However, these thoughts quickly turn into motivation for my own progress and integration into the team.I collected a lot of materials in books and the Internet to learn and organize knowledge.Through frequent problem exchanges with others, I quickly adapted to the new environment.
  • I would like to say that my greatest strengths are being thoughtful and responsible in my work. For instance, in one of our projects involving an alarm feature, I repeatedly confirmed the functionality of the server or PLC (used for data acquisition) in different situations such as network disconnection, power failure, or program crashes to ensure that we provide accurate and reliable information to the customer.

Why should we hire you？
我具有高度的责任感和良好的适应能力，同时我很喜欢探索新的领域，擅长简化复杂问题和刨根问底，我认为我会成为具有良好业务能力且值得信赖的同事。

项目经历

What does the project you do have to do with software development?
我的项目都是一些物联网应用方向的，这些项目的主要目的都是通过我们服务器中的软件服务与硬件采集器进行实时通讯，进而实现对数据的采集和对生产过程的控制。在项目中，软件部分是由我和我的团队负责的，我们通过与硬件合作方交流共同协商数据传输的相关细节，例如接口内容等等，共同完成项目。

Why do such a project, what is the point and innovation?

公司与职业规划

Optiver

Can you tell me how you know this position？
I learned about this position through a search engine. In fact, I have been searching for fintech-related positions recently, hoping to gain insights into how IT technology is applied in trading businesses, such as improving the success rate of transactions. Additionally, I believe that on a global platform like Optiver, I can face and solve more challenges.

What attracted you to our company?
What attracts me most is your company’s mission - “we improve the market”. By browsing your website, I know that your company is a responsible and trustworthy enterprise that provides stable services to its customers. I think that if I could learn at your company, it could also help me develop these characteristics and assist me in my personal growth. In addition, Optiver’s global business and international work environment can also provide me with a broader perspective.

特点

Optiver is a global market maker

业务

We provide institutional investors around the globe with liquidity in equities, FX, fixed income and commodity products.

责任

==Consistent liquidity==：Markets need liquidity to function effectively. We trade our own capital at our own risk to provide just that.
==Globally trusted==：Working with exchanges across the globe, we provide continuous prices for thousands of financial instruments.
==A stabilising force==：No matter the market conditions, we stay the course.
