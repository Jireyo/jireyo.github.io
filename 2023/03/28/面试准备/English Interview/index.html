自我介绍

校园背景

自我介绍
Tell me sth about you？

  • My name is Jin Renyu.From Huazhong University of Science and technology. I’m studying for my master’s degree in mechanical engineering. I am expected to graduate in June 2024.
  • After studying in Central South University for 4 years, I got the quali (call ni) fication to be recommended to my graduate school. In junior, I discovered my interest in coding. Compared to other courses that required extensive experimental conditions, coding allowed me to easily and quickly bring my ideas to life, which is why I started dedicating time to learning programming.
  • In graduate school, I worked with my fellow students in the lab to complete several Internet of Things projects, which benefit me a lot.

为什么没学计算机？
Why didn’t you get a master’s in computer science?

  • Because I had already got the qualification to be recommended to my graduate school, taking the postgraduate entrance examination for computer science was not a cost-effective option at that time.

为什么想转行？
Which course did you find it difficult to obtain experimental conditions？

  • There was a course called “Digital Design of ElectroMechanical Products” in which my team and I designed an electric wheelchair. We designed the structure and did some calculations to make it stable in multiple gears. Unfortunately, it was only a design and we didn’t have the means to manufacture or test it.

为什么对编程感兴趣？
How did you get interested in programming?

  • One day, I found out that one of my classmates had created his own learning blog. I was inspired and wanted to have a personal webpage that I could freely customize, so I started learning programming.

学得最好的学科
What was your best course of study?

  • The best course I learned during my graduate studies was “Management Information Systems Analysis and Design.” I enjoy courses with clear objectives, and through studying this course, I improved the processes and details that I had overlooked in past development.
  • In that class, I implemented a course selection system!

学得最差的学科
What was your worst course of study?

  • My worst course was linear（内你儿） algebra（奥几把）. At first I had trouble accepting the abstract concepts, and I got poor grades in this course, which made me very depressed. Later, I understood its principle by searching teaching videos on the Internet(I remember the author is from youtuber and is named 3Blue1Brown ), after that, I re-reading the textbook systematically and mastered these knowledge.

个人优势

What is your greatest strength？

  • I would like to say that my biggest strength is my strong ability to self-study and adapt. When I enter a new ==environment==, like when I first joined the lab at graduate school , I often feel uncomfortable because I worry about not being able to keep up with others’ progress or being accepted by them. However, these thoughts quickly turn into motivation for my own progress and integration into the team. I collected a lot of materials in books and the Internet to learn and organize knowledge.Through frequent problem exchanges with others, I quickly adapted to the new environment.
  • I would like to say that | my greatest strengths are being thoughtful and | responsible in my work. For instance, in one of my projects involving an alarm feature, I repeatedly confirmed the ==functionality== of the server or PLC (used for data ==acquisition==(啊亏贼显)) in different situations such as network disconnection, power failure, or program crashes to ensure that we provide ==accurate== and reliable information to the customer.

Why should we hire you？

  • I have a high sense of responsibility and i’m very adaptable. At the same time, I like to explore new fields, and I am good at simplifying complex problems and getting to the bottom of them. I believe I will become a trustworthy colleague (call 你哥) with good business ability.

个人劣势

Why java？

  • Java is a widely used programming language.
  • It has many tools and java has a good design pattern and object-oriented features.I can quickly build a web application using Java language.
  • Of course, I also know that due to its running on a virtual machine and automatic garbage collection, Java’s performance may not be as high as languages like C++.

你最大的缺点
How would you handle a stuation where you and your supervisor disagreed about an issue or course of action?

  • My biggest weakness is public speaking, especially when I am facing a group of strangers, my mind tends to be blank. However, I realize that this is an essential skill in the workplace, so I started to make changes. I often do a lot of preparation before the speech,so that I don’t get stuck due to the content during the speech, and can focus more on how to perform better. Now, I can confidently express my ideas in front of strangers, even though nervousness may still occur sometimes, I believe that with practice and experience, I will do better and better.

挫折

挫折
When have you suffered a setback?

  • The major setback I suffered in the project was during the implementation process, where the data collection program would sometimes crash, causing the system to fail.
  • This event never occurred（e可儿） in our testing, and even when I restarted the program, the crashes still occurred from time to time. I needed to solve the problem as quickly as possible to prevent further data loss.It made me nervous and anxious.
  • Through comparison（抗怕瑞森）, I found that the main problem occurred during the connection establishment process with the PLC in the production environment. in special, a section of communication-related code in the dependent library did not have a null pointer condition check.
  • I made modifications to the code and resolved the problem, preventing data loss. I also informed the original author of the bug and made updates to prevent others from encountering the same problem.

项目经历

What does the project you do have to do with software development?

  • My projects are focused on applications related to the Internet of Things. Whether it’s projects related to tobacco or traditional manufacturing, the main goal is to achieve real-time communication through software services and hardware collectors, in order to collect data and control the production process. In the project, my team is responsible for the software part, and we work with the hardware partner to discuss and negotiate the details of data transmission, such as interface content.

细节

Why do such a project, what is the point and innovation?
Intelligent cigar drying cloud platform system based on Internet of Things

  • We did this project because our lab has a partnership with the Tobacco Science Institute.
  • For example, in the cigar project, because the production of cigar drying is generally carried out in remote and harsh environments, the current production mode is for cigar farmers to set the drying controller based on their personal experience, resulting in uneven product quality.
  • Our target is to reduce the labor cost of this process and improve product quality by monitoring the process. (Specific tasks include collecting environmental data during cigar drying, such as temperature, humidity, and some image information of cigar leaves. Upload these data to the server for analysis. Usually, we can find the best process curve for a certain type of cigar. By comparing the collected production data with the best curve, we can adjust the target temperature setting of the drying room controller to make it close to the ideal situation, thereby improving the quality and economic value of the product.)
  • I am mainly responsible for the writing of back-end code and the data acquisition(啊亏贼显) interfaces

Wuhan newwish technology equipment information management system

  • For example, I did a project during my internship.We do this project because it is one of our research topics. We need to build an information platform to collect production information in the manufacturing process for research and analysis.
  • The main purpose of the project was to establish an information system for a forging production line that had already been automated. By collecting equipment and production information such as speed, energy consumption, and production pace during the process, we stored this data in a database and displayed it on a large screen on site through a web application. Enterprise employees can clearly understand the current health status of the equipment and whether there are any malfunctions that need to be addressed. This information can guide them to better and quickly respond to equipment failures, save energy costs, and increase production output.

Technology stack

  • Our project is mainly written in java language, and the framework of background-end is Spring and Mybatis.
  • The database uses mysql and redis for caching the real time.
  • The main framework for the front-end is vue.
  • The front and back end can communicate with each other through websocket, which realizes real-time data refresh.
  • By using the echarts component to present the data we’ve collected, we can show our customers clearly what’s going on.

  • Knowleage: HTTP、TCP/IP protocol、Data structures and algorithms
